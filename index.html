Kartik LakshminarasimhanResearcher at imec

Education

I am a researcher at CSA department at imec. Previously, I was a PhD Candidate in Computer Science and Engineering at Ghent University and a member of Performance Lab where I was advised by Prof. Lieven Eeckhout. I have a Master's from the Univesity of Connecticut. I graduated with a B.Eng. in Electronics and Communication at SRM EEC, Anna University.

Research

I work on GPU and Accelerator microarchitectures on the context of LLMs. At Ghent University, I worked on Complexity-effective general-purpose processor microarchitectures which are suitable for edge devices. Also, I am interested in characterizing Deep Learning applications. Prior to joining UGent, I was a research intern at Intel Microachitecture Research Labs, Bangalore, where I created a tool visualizing the critical path of micro-ops executing on an OOO CPU model.
